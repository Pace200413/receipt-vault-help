ReceiptVault • Last updated: February 2026

ReceiptVault is designed to be private by default. Your receipts are stored on your device. We do not sell your data.

Data we collect

  • Receipt content (merchant, amount, date, category, notes) you add — stored on-device.
  • Receipt photos you attach or scan (optional) — stored on-device.
  • Subscription status (to unlock Pro features) — handled by Apple via StoreKit.

How we use data

Data is used only to provide app features such as scanning, organizing, insights, reminders, and exporting.

Third-party services

Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple. ReceiptVault does not receive your payment card details.

Note: If you enable reminders, iOS notifications are used only for alerts you choose. If you import/scan receipts, the app may request Photos/Camera permission.

Questions? Email us at receiptvaulthelp@gmail.com.

This page is provided for transparency and to satisfy App Store requirements. If our practices change, we will update this policy.
