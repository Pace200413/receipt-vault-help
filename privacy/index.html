Privacy Policy — ReceiptVault

Last updated: February 2026

ReceiptVault is designed to store your receipts on your device. We do not sell your data.

Data We Collect

How Data Is Used

Data is used only to provide app features (receipt storage, search, export, reminders).

Third-Party Services

Subscriptions are processed by Apple. We do not receive your payment details.
